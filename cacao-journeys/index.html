Agroverse Cacao Journeys

Drifts connecting communities, farms, and the regenerative journey of cacao. These journeys invite you to wander with intention, connecting with the land, the people, and the transformative power of regenerative cacao.

Each journey offers a unique drift of discovery. Whether you're wandering through gathering spaces along the Pacific West Coast where communities come together, or visiting the farms in Brazil where regenerative cacao grows, these drifts invite mindful connection and conscious transformation.

The Pacific West Coast Drift

Journey from Seattle to the California desert, visiting partner venues where communities gather around regenerative cacao. Experience the diverse spaces—from urban sanctuaries to desert retreats—where our cacao brings people together in mindful connection.

The Brazilian Drift

Embark on a journey to visit the regenerative cacao farms in Brazil. Starting from Founderhaus in Florianópolis, travel north through the farms of Bahia to Pará, walking the land where our cacao grows, meeting the farmers, and witnessing firsthand the regenerative practices that restore the rainforest while supporting local communities.
