Itacaré Cultural Immersion

Capoeira, Samba & Forró in Bahia

About This Experience

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Bahia in Itacaré, where Afro-Brazilian traditions come alive through movement, music, and community. This coastal town offers a perfect blend of beach culture, traditional arts, and the warm hospitality that defines Bahia.

Capoeira at Tribo Bahia by Bico duro: Learn this Afro-Brazilian martial art that combines dance, acrobatics, and music. Capoeira is more than a physical practice—it's a cultural expression with deep roots in resistance and community. At Tribo Bahia, you'll experience authentic capoeira instruction that connects you to this powerful tradition.

Street Samba: As evening falls, the cobblestone streets of Itacaré come alive with the rhythm of samba. Join locals and travelers alike as spontaneous samba circles form, creating moments of pure joy and connection. The energy is infectious, and everyone is welcome to join the dance.

Forró Dancing: Experience forró, a traditional Brazilian dance style that brings communities together in celebration. With its distinctive accordion-driven rhythm, forró creates an atmosphere of warmth and connection that embodies the spirit of Bahia.

These cultural experiences in Itacaré offer a deeper understanding of the Afro-Brazilian heritage that shapes Bahia's identity, connecting you to the rhythms, movements, and community spirit that make this region so special.
