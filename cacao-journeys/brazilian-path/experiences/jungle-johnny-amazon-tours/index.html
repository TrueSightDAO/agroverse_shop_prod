Jungle Johnny Amazon Tours

Authentic Amazon Rainforest Adventure

About This Experience

Embark on an authentic Amazon rainforest adventure with Jungle Johnny Tours, starting from Manaus. This immersive experience takes you deep into the heart of the Amazon, where you'll witness the incredible biodiversity and learn about the ecosystem that our regenerative cacao farms help protect and restore.

Flooded Forests & Pink River Dolphins: Navigate through the unique flooded forests (várzea) of the Amazon, where trees stand in water during the rainy season. Spot pink river dolphins playing in the dark waters of the Rio Negro, and learn about the complex aquatic ecosystems that support life in the Amazon.

Medicinal Plants & Indigenous Knowledge: Discover the medicinal plants that have been used by indigenous communities for centuries. Your guide will share knowledge about traditional healing practices and the importance of preserving this wisdom for future generations.

Jungle Lodges: Sleep in authentic jungle lodges, falling asleep to the sounds of the rainforest—the calls of howler monkeys, the chorus of frogs, and the rustling of leaves. Wake up to the dawn chorus of birds and the mist rising from the forest floor.

Piranha Fishing: Experience the thrill of fishing for piranhas in the Amazon's tributaries. Learn about these fascinating fish and their role in the ecosystem, then enjoy a traditional Amazonian meal prepared with your catch.

Meeting of the Waters: Witness one of nature's most spectacular phenomena—where the dark Rio Negro and the sandy-colored Solimões River flow side by side for miles without mixing. This natural wonder occurs due to differences in temperature, speed, and density between the two rivers.

Connection to Regenerative Cacao: This experience connects you deeply with the Amazon ecosystem that our regenerative cacao farms help protect. Understanding the rainforest's complexity and fragility reinforces the importance of regenerative agriculture practices that restore rather than deplete the land.

Jungle Johnny Tours offers an authentic, respectful approach to Amazon exploration, working with local communities and prioritizing conservation. After this adventure, you'll continue your journey with a cargo boat trip upriver to Leticia.
