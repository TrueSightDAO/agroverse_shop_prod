About CEPOTX

CEPOTX is a cooperative that supports small-scale cacao farmers in Pará, Brazil, located in the heart of the Amazon Rainforest. The cooperative provides essential resources, knowledge sharing, and market access to help member farms maintain the highest standards of quality and sustainability.

Through CEPOTX, small-scale farmers gain access to regenerative farming practices, quality control systems, and fair trade opportunities. The cooperative's mission is to empower farmers while preserving and enhancing the Amazon Rainforest ecosystem through sustainable agroforestry practices.

Member farms of CEPOTX produce award-winning cacao that reflects the unique terroir of the Amazon Rainforest. The cooperative's commitment to regenerative agriculture ensures that each harvest contributes to the health of the rainforest while providing sustainable livelihoods for farming families.
