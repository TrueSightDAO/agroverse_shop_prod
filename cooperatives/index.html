Our Cooperatives

Cooperatives play a vital role in supporting small-scale farmers, providing resources, knowledge sharing, and market access. Learn about the cooperatives that work with our partner farms to produce regenerative cacao.

Our partner farms work with cooperatives that support regenerative agriculture, quality standards, and sustainable livelihoods. These cooperatives enable small-scale farmers to access resources, share knowledge, and maintain the highest standards of quality while preserving traditional farming methods.

CEPOTX

CEPOTX supports small-scale cacao farmers in Pará, Brazil, through regenerative practices. Member farms like Paulo's La do Sitio produce award-winning Amazon Rainforest cacao.
