Agroverse Partners with Mestre Bico Duro to Bring Capoeira Fitness and Cacao Circle Gatherings to the USA

Published: November 22, 2024 By TrueSight Community

We're thrilled to announce an exciting new partnership between Agroverse and Mestre Bico Duro! This collaboration brings together the world of regenerative agriculture, Capoeira, and community-building.

Mestre Bico Duro has crafted a unique series of fitness programs inspired by the ancient Brazilian martial art of Capoeira. These programs will be integrated into our upcoming cacao circle gatherings in the USA.

Starting soon, Agroverse will host 1.5-hour cacao circle gatherings, where participants can experience the transformative power of Capoeira while enjoying ceremonial-grade cacao tea made from Amazonian cacao husk.

Donation-Based and Supporting a Great Cause

These gatherings will operate on a donation-based model, with all contributions supporting the Tribo Bahia Mirim program in Itacare, Bahia, Brazil. This initiative provides after-school education programs for 47 children, focusing on traditional Capoeira, English, and arts and crafts.

Regeneratively Grown Amazonian Cacao Nibs

During these gatherings, participants will also have the opportunity to purchase regeneratively grown Amazonian cacao nibs. The proceeds from these sales will support farmers restoring the Amazonian rainforest through traditional agroforestry practices known as Cabruca.

Join the Movement

We invite you to be part of this unique experience, combining physical movement, community connection, and regenerative practices. Stay tuned for updates on our upcoming cacao circle gatherings and Capoeira fitness programs!

Together, let's cultivate a deeper connection with ourselves, our communities, and the natural world.
