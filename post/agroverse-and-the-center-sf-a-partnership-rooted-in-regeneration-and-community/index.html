Agroverse and The Center SF: A Partnership Rooted in Regeneration and Community

Published: January 10, 2025 By TrueSight Community


Agroverse is excited to announce our partnership with The Center SF, a community hub dedicated to personal, spiritual, and social transformation. Together, we're bringing regenerative Amazonian cacao nibs to the San Francisco community, fostering a connection between the vibrant city and the lush rainforest.


The Center SF is renowned for its commitment to fostering positive change through tea, movement, art, and community. This alignment of values makes our partnership a natural fit, as both Agroverse and The Center SF prioritize transformation, well-being, and sustainability.


Agroverse's regenerative cacao nibs are sourced from small farmers in the Amazon rainforest who practice agroforestry, a sustainable farming method that revitalizes the environment and supports local communities. By choosing our cacao nibs, The Center SF's patrons become part of an impactful mission to restore the Amazon and uplift the people who call it home.


Our cacao nibs are not only a catalyst for change but also a delicious, nutrient-rich addition to your diet. Packed with antioxidants, magnesium, and iron, these nibs offer a healthy boost to smoothies, baked goods, and snacks.


Now available for purchase at The Center SF, you can experience the transformative power of Agroverse's regenerative cacao nibs firsthand. Visit The Center SF to taste the rich, chocolatey flavors of the Amazon and support a brighter future for our planet and its people.


We are grateful for the opportunity to partner with The Center SF and share our passion for regeneration and community. Together, we can restore our Amazon rainforest, one cacao at a time.
