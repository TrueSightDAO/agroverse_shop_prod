About Kiki's Cocoa

Kiki's Cocoa is a boutique San Francisco chocolatier crafting exquisite, ethically-sourced confections, offering monthly subscriptions, single-origin ganache, and truffle-making classes for a delightful chocolate experience.

As a partner venue, Kiki's Cocoa proudly offers Agroverse ceremonial cacao, connecting their community with regenerative, ethically-sourced cacao from Brazilian farmers. This partnership represents our shared commitment to sustainability, community connection, and the transformative power of ceremonial cacao.
