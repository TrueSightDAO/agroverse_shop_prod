About Lumin Earth Apothecary

Lumin Earth Apothecary is more than just a shop—it's an experience. Owned by mother and daughter Summer and Sierra, this herbal sanctuary, crystal shop, and house-plant haven offers a place to nourish your body, mind, and spirit. Within their space, you'll find Lumin's Earths Tea Bar where Summer and Sierra craft made-to-order herbal elixirs, tea lattes, and teas for you to sip and enjoy.

Explore their selection of bulk herbs, high-vibrational crystals, rare houseplants, and their handcrafted line of herbal remedies, including tinctures and tea blends designed to support your well-being. Whether you're there for a cup of tea, the perfect crystal, or to immerse yourself in earth's medicine, Summer and Sierra invite you to experience the magic of their space.

They also offer soul readings and herbal guidance, creating a holistic approach to wellness that aligns perfectly with Agroverse's mission of regenerative, ethically-sourced cacao. Find our regenerative cacao products at their beautiful Morro Bay location.
