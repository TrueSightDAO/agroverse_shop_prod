About Embodied Blindfold Dance

Embodied Blindfold Dance offers transformative movement experiences that combine dance, mindfulness, and sensory exploration. Through blindfolded movement practices, participants are invited to connect more deeply with their bodies, release inhibitions, and explore movement in new and profound ways.

As an Agroverse partner, Embodied Blindfold Dance incorporates our regenerative cacao into their healing and movement workshops, creating spaces for deep connection, personal transformation, and embodied awareness. The combination of mindful movement and ceremonial cacao creates a powerful container for healing and self-discovery.

Whether you're seeking to deepen your movement practice, explore new ways of connecting with your body, or experience the healing power of dance and cacao together, Embodied Blindfold Dance offers a unique and transformative journey.
