About Hacker Dojo

Hacker Dojo is a collaborative hackerspace located in Mountain View, California, established in 2009. It serves as a community center for entrepreneurs, programmers, and creators, providing a space to learn, collaborate, and innovate. The Dojo fosters a culture of open collaboration, knowledge sharing, and creative problem-solving.

As a vibrant hub for the tech community in Silicon Valley, Hacker Dojo hosts workshops, meetups, hackathons, and events that bring together innovators from diverse backgrounds. The space embodies the spirit of open-source collaboration and entrepreneurial energy that defines the Bay Area tech scene.

Agroverse is proud to partner with Hacker Dojo, bringing our regenerative, ethically-sourced cacao to this innovative community space. Whether you're attending a workshop, working on a project, or connecting with fellow creators, you can experience our premium cacao at Hacker Dojo.
