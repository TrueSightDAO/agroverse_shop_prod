The Farm Story

Fazenda Analuana, operated by Ana Luana, is a regenerative cacao farm in Bahia, Brazil, dedicated to sustainable farming practices and producing high-quality cacao products. The farm represents the commitment of small-scale farmers to regenerative agriculture and the production of unique cacao products like cacao molasses.

Ana Luana's farm specializes in producing cacao molasses‚Äîa unique product that showcases the versatility of cacao beyond traditional beans and nibs. This innovative product demonstrates the farm's commitment to value-added processing and creating sustainable livelihoods through regenerative cacao farming.

Through regenerative practices, Fazenda Analuana contributes to the restoration and preservation of Bahia's agricultural landscape while producing exceptional cacao products. The farm's work supports both environmental sustainability and economic viability for small-scale farmers in the region.

Fazenda Analuana's partnership with Agroverse through shipments AGL5 and AGL7 demonstrates the farm's commitment to transparency, quality, and sustainable production. These shipments of cacao molasses represent the farm's innovative approach to cacao processing and their role in supporting regenerative agriculture in Bahia.
